* Unicode-support
	- Currently requires PHP 5.2.2 (current) or older to be configured with:
		mbstring.func_overload = 2
		or similar, to turn on mb function overloading for str* functions
		PHP 6 will come with full unicode support in the core, so hopefully this can be deprecated soon
